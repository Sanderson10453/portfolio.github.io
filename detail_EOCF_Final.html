Entrepreneurs of Color Fund Dashboards

I manage the dashboard for the Entrepreneurs of Color Fund. The dashboard displays how the $90+ million that LISC & JP-Morgan haved investmented uplifts small businesses of color and centers equity.

Investing in small businesses of color can diminish historical inequities

The Entrepreneurs of Color Fund (EOCF) makes grants and provides technical assistance to small businesses across the nation to ensure their success. The fund focuses on small businesses that are owned by Black, Latinx/Hispanic, Indigenous, and Asian people. Using ETL flows, I manage the dashboard that updates JP Morgan-Chase and LISC on the progress of the fund and how many small businesses of color have been invested in.
