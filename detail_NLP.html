Natural Language Processing Survey Assessment

Using neural networks with Tensorflow to assess the wording of survey questions

Leveraging the power of natural language processing and neural networks to flag unclear survey questions

I created a neural network model that allows me to quickly review survey questions to assess if questions are well worded or need adjustment. Through this model, the time required for our team to go over surveys can be cut down as there's only a need to go over flagged questions.

Process

The first photo shows a survey we created to collect information for the Digital Navigator program - a program that allows us to increase the digital literacy of clients. As a multi-team iniative, there were many players contributing questions and it was my job to review questions and make suggestions. To improve this process, I created a neural network model that would read these new questions and highlight questions that may need more work.

The third photo shows the basic keras model that was used to read the sequences, including the number of neurons and the loss function. Once compiled, we could train and test this model using our training and test data.
